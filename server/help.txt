`verify` - Used to verify and start the game
`shop` -  Used to display the shop!
`hunt` - Used to hunt monsters!
`status` - Used to check your health and status
`description` - Used to change the description for your status and profile
`profile` - Used to display armor and other stats
`buy` - Used to buy items from the shop
`heal` - Used to replenish your health. Make sure to have a stable amount of health_potions!
`adventure` - Used to go adventure!
Adventuring is very dangerous and can result in your death!
The drops are good and there is more money and XP to be found!
